About the Project
Why OquMi?
In Kazakhstan, thousands of students take the Comprehensive Testing (CT) exam annually to enter master's degree programs. However, there are almost no quality online resources for systematic preparation.

OquMi solves this problem: the platform combines structured courses, interactive quizzes, and a full-format mock exam with a countdown timer and automatic result checking.

Students receive personal progress analytics, identify their weak areas, and can purposefully focus on the topics they need most.
